About Amazon

Overview

Amazon stands as a titan in the realms of e-commerce and cloud computing, fundamentally reshaping industries and consumer behavior on a global scale. With a pioneering spirit that spans more than two decades, Amazon continues to redefine customer expectations and operational efficiency across its diverse portfolio. Leveraging unparalleled scale and logistical expertise, Amazon delivers seamless shopping experiences and innovative cloud solutions that empower businesses worldwide. By harnessing cutting-edge technologies such as AI and machine learning, Amazon drives transformative initiatives that set new benchmarks in digital commerce and cloud services. Amazon's relentless commitment to innovation and customer-centric solutions underscores its leadership in driving forward digital transformation at a monumental scale.

Industry

E-commerce, Cloud Computing, Artificial Intelligence

Company Size

Amazon's workforce comprises over 1,521,000 million employees, forming a diverse talent pool dedicated to innovation and operational excellence.

Product, Services, & Operations

Amazon offers a comprehensive range of products and services including e-commerce retail, Amazon Web Services (AWS), digital streaming, logistics and fulfillment services, Alexa artificial intelligence, and pioneering hardware solutions like Kindle and Echo.

Awards & Achievements

Amazon's groundbreaking achievements and industry recognition reinforce its role as a global innovator in e-commerce, cloud computing, and digital technology.

Global Presence

Operating across more than 20 countries, Amazon's expansive global footprint serves millions of customers and businesses, driving growth and operational efficiency on a global scale.

Revenue

As a publicly traded company (NASDAQ: AMZN), in the fiscal year ended March 31, 2024, Amazon generated consolidated revenues of US $590.740 billion, showcasing its financial prowess.
