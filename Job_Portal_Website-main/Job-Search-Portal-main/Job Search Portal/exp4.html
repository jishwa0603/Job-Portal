About Apple Inc.

Company Overview

Apple Inc., founded by Steve Jobs, Steve Wozniak, and Ronald Wayne in 1976, is one of the most valuable companies in the world, with a market valuation exceeding $2 trillion. Known for its innovative products and cutting-edge technology, Apple has consistently set the benchmark in the tech industry.

Industry

Technology

Company Size

Apple operates in over 500 offices worldwide and employs more than 147,000 people globally.

Products, Services, and Operations

  • iPhone: A leading smartphone known for its design, performance, and advanced features.
  • Mac: A series of personal computers and laptops.
  • iPad: A versatile tablet suitable for both professional and personal use.
  • Apple Watch: A smartwatch that integrates health tracking and connectivity.
  • Apple TV: A digital media player and streaming device.
  • Services: Includes Apple Music, iCloud, Apple Pay, and the App Store.

Acquisitions

  • Beats Electronics (2014): Acquired for $3 billion, this purchase helped Apple enhance its audio technology and launch Apple Music.
  • Shazam (2018): Bought for $400 million, this acquisition improved Apple's music recognition capabilities.
  • Intel's Smartphone Modem Business (2019): Acquired for $1 billion, this acquisition helped Apple develop its own modem technology for future devices.

These acquisitions have significantly contributed to Apple's revenue, which reached $365 billion in 2021.
