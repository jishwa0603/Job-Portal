About HCL

HCL Technologies

HCL Technologies is a leading global IT services company that helps enterprises reimagine their businesses for the digital age. Founded in 1976 by Shiv Nadar, HCL Technologies has grown to become a prominent player in the IT industry. With a market valuation of approximately $40 billion as of 2023, HCL Technologies offers an extensive range of services and solutions to clients around the world.

Industry

Information Technology and Services

Company Size

HCL Technologies operates in over 50 countries with more than 225,000 employees globally, solidifying its presence in key international markets.

Products, Services, and Operations

  • IT and Business Services: Application development, systems integration, and IT infrastructure management.
  • Engineering and R&D Services: Product engineering, platform engineering, and IoT solutions.
  • Digital Process Operations: Business process outsourcing, supply chain management, and finance and accounting services.
  • Digital Foundation: Cloud services, cybersecurity, and digital workplace solutions.

Acquisitions

HCL Technologies has made several strategic acquisitions to enhance its service offerings and expand its global footprint. Some of the notable acquisitions include:

  • Digital Transformation: In 2018, HCL acquired Actian Corporation, a hybrid data management, analytics, and integration company, to strengthen its data management and analytics capabilities.
  • Security Services: The acquisition of AppScan from IBM in 2019 bolstered HCL’s cybersecurity services portfolio, particularly in application security testing.
  • Engineering Services: In 2020, HCL acquired Cisco’s Self-Optimizing Network (SON) technology to enhance its engineering and R&D services, particularly in the telecommunications sector.

These acquisitions have significantly contributed to HCL Technologies' growth, with the company generating an annual revenue of over $12 billion.
