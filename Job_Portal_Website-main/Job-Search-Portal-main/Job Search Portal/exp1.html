About Infosys

Company Overview

Infosys is a global leader in next-generation digital services and consulting. Founded in 1981 by N. R. Narayana Murthy and six other engineers, Infosys has grown to become a beacon of technological excellence. With a market valuation of approximately $82 billion as of 2023, Infosys consistently delivers end-to-end solutions that transform and empower businesses worldwide.

Industry

Information Technology and Services

Company Size

Infosys operates in more than 50 countries with over 250 offices globally. The company employs approximately 343,000 professionals across various disciplines, establishing itself as a major player in the IT industry.

Products, Services, and Operations

  • Digital Services: Cloud computing, AI, big data, IoT, and blockchain.
  • Consulting Services: IT strategy, digital transformation, and business consulting.
  • Engineering Services: Product engineering, digital engineering, and systems engineering.
  • Application Services: Application development, modernization, and maintenance.
  • Business Process Management: Finance and accounting, sourcing and procurement, and customer service management.

Acquisitions

  • Digital and Creative Services: In 2018, Infosys acquired Wongdoody, a US-based creative and consumer insights agency, to strengthen its digital and creative capabilities.
  • Cloud and Digital Transformation: The acquisition of Fluido in 2018, a leading Salesforce consulting partner in Nordics, enabled Infosys to boost its cloud and digital transformation services in Europe.
  • Enterprise Service Management: Infosys acquired Simplus in 2020, a leading Salesforce consulting and advisory firm, to expand its Salesforce enterprise service offerings.

These acquisitions have significantly contributed to Infosys's growth, with the company generating an annual revenue of over $17 billion.

Summary
