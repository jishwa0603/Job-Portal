Company Overview - Salesforce & KPMG

Salesforce Overview

Salesforce is a global leader in customer relationship management (CRM) software, headquartered in San Francisco, California. It was founded in 1999 by Marc Benioff, Parker Harris, Dave Moellenhoff, and Frank Dominguez. Salesforce provides a suite of enterprise applications focused on customer service, marketing automation, analytics, and application development. As of 2023, Salesforce is valued at approximately $220 billion.

Industry

Salesforce operates within the Cloud Computing and Enterprise Software industry.

Company Size

Salesforce has a substantial global presence with over 70 offices in more than 30 countries. The company employs approximately 75,000 professionals, making it a significant player in the cloud computing and CRM sectors.

Products, Services, and Operations

  • Sales Cloud
  • Service Cloud
  • Marketing Cloud
  • Commerce Cloud
  • Tableau (Analytics)
  • MuleSoft (Integration Platform)

Their most popular offerings include Salesforce CRM, which helps businesses manage customer relationships, and Tableau for powerful data visualization and analytics.

KPMG Overview

KPMG (Klynveld Peat Marwick Goerdeler) is one of the leading global professional services firms, offering audit, tax, and advisory services. Headquartered in Amstelveen, Netherlands, KPMG was founded in 1987 through the merger of Klynveld Main Goerdeler (KMG) and Peat Marwick International. As of 2023, KPMG is valued at approximately $35 billion. The firm is known for its commitment to quality and exceptional client service, helping businesses navigate complex challenges and achieve sustainable growth.

Industry

KPMG operates within the Professional Services industry.

Company Size

KPMG has a significant global presence with over 650 offices in 147 countries. The firm employs around 236,000 professionals, making it one of the largest employers in the professional services sector.

Products, Services, and Operations

  • Audit and Assurance Services
  • Tax Services
  • Advisory Services
  • Risk Consulting
  • Management Consulting

Their most popular offerings include comprehensive audit services, strategic tax planning, and advisory services that help organizations improve their performance and manage risks.
