About Google

Company Overview

Google is a global technology company that specializes in internet-related services and products. Founded in 1998 by Larry Page and Sergey Brin, Google has grown to become a cornerstone of the digital age, known for its search engine, advertising services, and numerous other technological innovations. With a market valuation of approximately $1.7 trillion as of 2023, Google is a leader in digital transformation and technological innovation.

Industry

Information Technology and Services

Company Size

Google operates in over 50 countries with more than 156,000 employees globally, establishing itself as one of the largest and most influential technology companies in the world.

Products, Services, and Operations

Google offers a vast array of products and services, including:

  • Search Engine: Google Search, the world’s most widely used search engine.
  • Advertising Services: Google Ads, AdSense, and Google Marketing Platform.
  • Cloud Computing: Google Cloud Platform (GCP), including infrastructure, platform, and software as a service (IaaS, PaaS, SaaS).
  • Software and Applications: Android OS, Google Chrome, Google Maps, Gmail, and Google Drive.
  • Hardware: Google Pixel phones, Google Nest smart home products, and Chromebook laptops.
  • Artificial Intelligence: AI and machine learning services through Google AI and TensorFlow.

Acquisitions

Google has made several strategic acquisitions to enhance its capabilities and expand its service offerings. Some of the notable acquisitions include:

  • Video Sharing Platform: In 2006, Google acquired YouTube for $1.65 billion, making it the leading platform for video content.
  • Mobile Advertising: The acquisition of AdMob in 2009 for $750 million helped Google strengthen its mobile advertising capabilities.
  • Artificial Intelligence: In 2014, Google acquired DeepMind, a British AI company, for $500 million, significantly boosting its AI and machine learning capabilities.

These acquisitions have significantly contributed to Google's growth, with the company generating an annual revenue of over $257 billion.
