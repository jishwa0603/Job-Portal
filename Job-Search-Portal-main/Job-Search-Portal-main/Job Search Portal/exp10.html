About Microsoft

About Company

Microsoft is a multinational technology company known for its software products, computing devices, and services. Founded in 1975 by Bill Gates and Paul Allen, Microsoft has grown to become one of the largest and most influential technology companies in the world. With a market valuation of approximately $2.5 trillion as of 2023, Microsoft continues to drive innovation across various technology domains.

Industry

Information Technology and Services

Company Size

Microsoft operates in over 190 countries with more than 181,000 employees globally, making it one of the largest technology employers worldwide.

Products, Services, and Operations

Microsoft provides a comprehensive range of products and services, including:

  • Software: Windows operating systems, Microsoft Office suite, and Microsoft Dynamics.
  • Cloud Services: Azure cloud computing services, including infrastructure, platform, and software as a service (IaaS, PaaS, SaaS).
  • Hardware: Surface devices, Xbox gaming consoles, and peripherals.
  • Artificial Intelligence: AI and machine learning solutions through Azure AI and other platforms.
  • Productivity Tools: Microsoft Teams, OneDrive, and SharePoint for collaboration and productivity.

Acquisitions

Microsoft has made several strategic acquisitions to enhance its capabilities and expand its service offerings. Some of the notable acquisitions include:

  • Professional Networking: In 2016, Microsoft acquired LinkedIn for $26.2 billion, significantly enhancing its professional networking and business solutions capabilities.
  • Cloud Computing: The acquisition of GitHub in 2018 for $7.5 billion strengthened Microsoft's position in cloud computing and software development.
  • Gaming and Entertainment: The acquisition of ZeniMax Media, the parent company of Bethesda Softworks, in 2021 for $7.5 billion, expanded Microsoft's gaming division and its Xbox Game Studios portfolio.

These acquisitions have significantly contributed to Microsoft's growth, with the company generating an annual revenue of over $168 billion.
