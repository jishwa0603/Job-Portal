About Accenture

Company Overview

Accenture is a global professional services company with leading capabilities in digital, cloud, and security. Founded in 1989 by Arthur E. Andersen, Accenture has grown to become a powerhouse in the consulting and technology services industry. With a market valuation of approximately $208 billion as of 2023, Accenture provides a wide range of services and solutions across more than 40 industries.

Industry

Professional Services and Information Technology

Company Size

Accenture operates in over 200 cities across 50 countries with more than 624,000 employees worldwide, making it one of the largest consulting firms globally.

Products, Services, and Operations

Accenture offers an extensive portfolio of products and services, including:

  • Strategy and Consulting: Business strategy, operations consulting, technology consulting, and management consulting.
  • Interactive: Digital marketing, customer experience, and design.
  • Technology: IT services, software development, cloud services, and cybersecurity.
  • Operations: Business process outsourcing, infrastructure services, and managed services.

Acquisitions

Accenture has made numerous strategic acquisitions to bolster its capabilities and expand its market reach. Some of the notable acquisitions include:

  • Digital Transformation: In 2019, Accenture acquired Droga5, a renowned creative agency, to enhance its creative and digital marketing capabilities.
  • Cybersecurity: The acquisition of Symantec's Cyber Security Services Business in 2020 helped Accenture strengthen its cybersecurity services portfolio.
  • Cloud Services: In 2021, Accenture acquired Infinity Works, a leading UK-based cloud and digital transformation consultancy, to expand its cloud services capabilities.

These acquisitions have significantly contributed to Accenture's growth, with the company generating an annual revenue of over $50 billion.
